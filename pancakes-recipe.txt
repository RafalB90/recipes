Ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/4 glass of milk
- 1 1/2 glass of flour
- 3 spoons of butter
- 1 little spoons of vanilla sugar
- 2 little spoons of sugar
- 3/4 spoon of baking soda
- pinch of salt

Directions:
1. Melt the butter.
2. Beat the eggs.
3. Add the rest of ingredients.
4. Mix everything up.
5. Leave for 15 minutes.
6. Fry on a pan.
7. Serve with maple syrup.
